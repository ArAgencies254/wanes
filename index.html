Our Products

Metro Credit

Metro Credit offers logbook loans where you can use your vehicle's logbook as collateral. To qualify, your vehicle must be fully owned and free of other loans. The process involves applying with the vehicle’s logbook and other documents, getting your vehicle appraised, and agreeing to loan terms, including repayment schedules and interest rates. If you default on the loan, the vehicle may be repossessed. Always review the terms carefully and understand all associated costs before committing.

TransitTAG

A transit tag is an electronic or physical identifier used to streamline and track movement through transit systems or checkpoints. It enhances efficiency by automating processes like toll payments or access control, and provides real-time tracking for goods, vehicles, or people.

Bundi Vehicle Tracking and Security

Bundi Vehicle Tracking and Security provides GPS-based solutions for monitoring and protecting vehicles, offering real-time location updates, theft prevention, and emergency response features to enhance security and fleet management.
